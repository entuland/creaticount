Creativerse Materials Counter

This tool lets you work out materials and time needed to craft / process / forge other items in the game Creativerse, free to play on Steam.

Check out the CreatiSign Generator to import your images into Arc Signs and the CreatiSprites Picker to easily choose and arrange sprites!

How to use this counter tool

Type part of the name of the item you need in the search box, then click on it to add it to the "Chosen items" list: once it's there enter the amount you "Need".

The "Preparation" section will show the items you have chosen with an additional "Possessed" number box (you can enter there the amount of items you have already placed in the BP or the amount of items you have already in your inventories, the Counter will tell you how many are missing considering that).

If an item has a recipe you can expand it in order to see what ingredients you need to craft them and how many you need in total. Those ingredients will be added to the "Preparation" section and if such a recipe involves forging or processing, the relative amount of time will be added to the total, depending on the amount of stations and the type of fuel you plan to use.

The amount of "Forge runs" refers to the total of separate jobs regardless of the fuel you chose, so you have to multiply that number if you're not going to use Coal or Tar food (which only consumes one unit of fuel per job).
